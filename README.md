# 📊 Childcare Cost Analysis Dashboard

---

# 🧠 Project Overview

Childcare costs in the United States have steadily increased over time, placing significant financial pressure on families. This project analyzes historical childcare cost data across U.S. states and counties to identify **cost trends, geographic disparities, and distribution patterns**.

The **Childcare Cost Analysis Dashboard** visualizes childcare affordability across states and highlights how costs have evolved from **2009–2018**.

The analysis shows that states such as **Massachusetts, California, Washington, and Connecticut consistently rank among the most expensive states for childcare services.**

---

# 🎯 Project Goals

This project was designed to:

- Analyze **childcare cost trends over time**
- Identify the **most expensive states for infant childcare**
- Examine **distribution patterns of childcare costs**
- Visualize **national cost trends**
- Provide insights into geographic childcare cost disparities

---

# 📊 Dashboard Visualizations

## 🔝 Top 10 Most Expensive States

The dashboard highlights states with the highest infant childcare costs.

Common high-cost states include:

- Massachusetts  
- California  
- Washington  
- Connecticut  
- Hawaii  

These states frequently appear among the highest childcare cost rankings across multiple years.

---

## 📉 Cost Distribution Analysis

Distribution histograms show how childcare costs vary across counties.

Key observations:

- Most counties fall within **$100–$200 average childcare cost range**
- Some counties exceed **$300–$400**, indicating significant regional disparities
- Cost distributions are **right-skewed**, showing a small number of extremely high-cost areas

---

## 📈 National Cost Trends

A time-series visualization shows a **steady upward trend in childcare costs over the decade**.

| Year | Mean Childcare Cost |
|-----|-----|
| 2009 | ~133 |
| 2012 | ~141 |
| 2015 | ~152 |
| 2018 | ~162 |

This trend highlights the growing financial burden childcare places on families.

---

# 🔍 Key Insights

### 📈 Rising Childcare Costs
Childcare costs have steadily increased nationwide over the 10-year period.

### 🗺 Geographic Cost Differences
States on the **West Coast and Northeast** consistently show higher childcare costs.

### 📊 Uneven Cost Distribution
County-level data reveals substantial variation in childcare affordability across the U.S.
